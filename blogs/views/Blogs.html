How to Choose the Right Wheelchair or Mobility Aid for Seniors?

Assess Mobility Level

The first step is evaluating the seniors current mobility and anticipated needs. This determines whether a manual or power wheelchair is most appropriate.

Manual wheelchairs are ideal for seniors who have assistance pushing or still have upper body strength. They have large rear wheels that the user propels themselves. Many are lightweight and foldable for transporting.

Power wheelchairs are best for those with limited arm and grip strength. The battery-powered motor assists movement using a joystick or other controls. Power chairs are heavier and may not fold.
